Pastor's welcome

Greenwood Community Covenant Church is now in its 82nd year of service. It is our privilege to have been called to be a pastor and wife team of this congregation. We have been in the ministry close to 50 years now, serving churches in Chicago, Illinois and in Alabama. Greenwood is a solid fellowship of believers. In today's world, you need a foundation - you need a family. You need someone that you can be yourself with. I have found that to be true of this fellowship. Please come and join us for worship.

God bless,

Bob, Mary & Matthew Simmons.

About our Sunday School

As with many congregations, today we have an aging population. Our desire is to bring more young people back into the church. We sponsor a yearly Vacation Bible School well attended, and endeavor to provide Sunday School Classes for all ages. We believe Sunday School has been dropped out of many churches these days, yet most people over the age of fifty, were probably taught by a faithful Sunday School Teacher and learned a great deal about God and the church in Sunday school.

We value that, and trying to see it strenghtened here at Greenwood. Actually, this fellowship began as a Sunday School outreach program, well over 80 years ago. We look forward trying to meet your needs, whatever they might be, and your family's opportunity for worship and education, and God's word.
